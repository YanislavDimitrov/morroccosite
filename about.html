Our Story

Welcome to Studio Morrocco, where creativity meets passion, and dreams transform into beautifully designed spaces!

Our story begins with a young girl who discovered her passion for interior design early on. She pursued her dream with dedication, earning a bachelor’s degree and driven by her dedication and love , she set out to find like-minded individuals who shared the same enthusiasm and creativity.

At Studio Morrocco, we believe in the power of collaboration and the magic that happens when talented and ambitious people come together. Our team is united by a shared commitment to excellence, innovation, and the belief that every project is an opportunity to create something extraordinary and learn something new.

Thank you for visiting our website. We invite you to explore our portfolio and join us on this exciting journey of design and creativity. Let's create something beautiful together.

What we offer:

Architecture

Taking measurements of the property using professional equipment.

Discussion

"Before the design process begins, a meeting is held to clarify the preliminary concept and establish the framework.

Layout

Proposal for isolating separate zones and positioning movable and fixed furniture.

3D Vizualization

Modeling the object and creating a realistic visualization of the property.

Technical Drawings

Preparation of drawings for the construction and installation works and furniture part.

Project Supervision

We organize, coordinate, and oversee the quality execution of renovation and installation activities, as well as the manufacturing of furniture.

We take pride in our ability to listen to our clients' needs and translate their visions into reality. Whether it's a cozy home, a chic apartment, or a functional office space, we approach each project with the same level of dedication and creativity.
