NT Apartment

Reduta, Sofia, Bulgaria

We are delighted to present a spacious and elegant apartment tailored for modern family living. This expansive layout includes a large living room seamlessly integrated with a kitchen and dining area, creating a central hub for family activities and entertaining.

The apartment features two thoughtfully designed children's rooms - one for a boy and one for a girl - each providing a cozy and personalized space. These rooms share a well-appointed bathroom, ensuring both convenience and comfort.

The master bedroom offers a private retreat with its own en-suite bathroom, providing luxury and privacy for the parents. Additionally, a generous vestibule enhances the overall flow and accessibility of the home.
