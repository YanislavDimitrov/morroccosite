IP Apartment

Lozenets, Sofia, Bulgaria
103 mm²

We present the interior design of a bright and spacious two-level apartment, created for a comfortable and modern lifestyle. The main idea of the project is the combination of functionality and aesthetics, with an emphasis on natural light and optimal use of space.

The large living room is the heart of the apartment, characterized by an open layout that creates a sense of spaciousness and freedom. The elegant staircase leading to the second level adds an architectural accent to the room.

On the second level is the beautiful bedroom with a sloped ceiling, offering a unique and intimate coziness.

The kitchen is functionally organized and extremely stylish, with direct access to a lovely balcony.

The home office is designed with productivity and comfort in mind, providing a calm environment for working from home.

This apartment combines contemporary design with functionality, offering a bright and spacious living area. Every detail is carefully selected to create a harmonious and pleasant environment for living and working.
